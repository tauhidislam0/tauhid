Education

I am very proud that I have completed my studies from a few such institutions. All the institutions were beautiful. I am actually very, very proud of my organizations.

Suti Dakhil Madrasa

I remember how long studied here for only 3 years. But there was a lot of flame here. The madrasa is next to my house, so when I was very young, I would go to the madrasa without wearing sandals. Again I would run home with the class fuck to eat. There are not many memories here. There is no such friend. However, some memories still float in the eyes.

Suti Model Government Primary School

Primary school was like the story of many foolish days. I still think I have to laugh. However, this happy has to be shared alone. Because many friends have been lost. Receiving here is getting a scholarship in the talent pool.



Suti H. S. S. Hight School

It is an organization that embodies many of the happiest and happiest memories of life. Even though the school was closed while studying here, I was not in a good mood. I thought I would go to school. How many boys play. How many stories. I mean, if there was another life like school life, then life would be full again. Many successes of life are coming in school life.

Agricultural University College

It was a traditional college in Bangladesh. It cannot be explained how much I am learning. In order to make life beautiful, Allah Almighty gave me the opportunity to study in such a college life. Whose teachers can not be finished talking here. However, college life was very paramount. When I was studying in college, I did not miss college much, but now I miss college very much. Including several Sir's classes.

Madhupur Higher Secondary College

In fact, I have to study in this college for 2 days, I could not even think in my life. However, the college was very beautiful to name. I thought I would study here from school. But whatever God puts in his forehead, he may do for good. I have never talked or met a friend like that here. Not a single memory. But there is my father and I still remember that smile with me.


Mawlana Bhashani Science & Technology University

Currently studying where. This is not the end of the story. A new turn in life at this university. Within a year, many happy and painful memories have become. However, when I was young, I used to hear that I did not study in the university. However, it is very fun to go to university. But I am proud to be able to study at a university.
